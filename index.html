📚 Coin Grading Quick Guide

G4 — Good: heavily worn, major design visible
VG8 — V.Good: well worn, main features clear
F12 — Fine: moderate wear, all lettering visible
VF20 — V.Fine: light-moderate wear on high points
EF40 — Ex.Fine: light wear on highest points only
AU50 — About Unc: slight wear on highest points
MS60 — Mint State: no wear, may have bag marks
MS63 — Choice MS: above avg luster, few marks
MS65 — Gem MS: strong luster, minor marks only
MS70 — Perfect: flawless under 5x magnification
